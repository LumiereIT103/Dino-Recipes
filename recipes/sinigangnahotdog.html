Sinigang na Hotdog

Sinigang na hotdog might sound unusual, but it's a delightful twist on a classic Filipino dish that your kids will surely love. The secret to our fun and kid-friendly recipe is the Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original which gives this dish its tangy and savory flavor. In less than 30 minutes, you can serve this warm bowl of sinigang na hotdog. Itâ€™s filled with nutritious veggies like tomatoes, okra, and onions that your kids won't even notice they're eating!

ingredients

  • 8 pcs hotdogs
  • 1 pack Knorr Sinigang Sa Sampalok mix
  • 2 pcs tomatoes, sliced in quarters
  • 6 pcs okra
  • 1 pc onion, quartered

Procedure

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Once boiling , add the onions and tomatoes.
  3. After five minutes, add the hotdogs, and okra.
  4. Mix in the Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix.
  5. Let it simmer for 15 minutes or until all the ingredients are cooked.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
